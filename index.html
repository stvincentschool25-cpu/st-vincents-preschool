Have Questions? We Have Answers!

Everything you need to know about joining St. Vincent's Preschool family.

What is the admission process?

Our admission process is simple! First, we recommend scheduling a campus tour to see our facility. You can then fill out the admission form, submit the required documents (like birth certificate and photos), and pay the admission fee to secure your child's spot. It’s that easy!

Book a Tour Now

What is your student-teacher ratio?

We pride ourselves on providing personalized attention. Our student-teacher ratio is kept low, typically around 10:1 for our Playgroup and Nursery classes. This ensures that every child receives the individual care and guidance they need to thrive.

See Our Programs

How do you ensure campus safety?

Your child's safety is our top priority. Our entire campus is under CCTV surveillance, and all our staff are trained in safety protocols. We have secure entry/exit points, child-proofed classrooms, and maintain a high standard of hygiene to create a safe and healthy learning environment.

Visit Our Safe Campus

What are the school timings?

Our school is open from 8:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Saturday. Our preschool programs (Playgroup, Nursery, LKG/UKG) typically run in the morning, while our full daycare services are available throughout the day to support working parents.
